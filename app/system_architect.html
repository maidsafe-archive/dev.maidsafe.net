What are we looking for?

  • You will have considerable experience of system level software implementation and design.
  • You will have experience of working in high impact, mission critical environments when results matter and exceeding expectations is the norm.
  • You will be an excellent communicator, providing clear and concise problem definitions as well as prompt resolutions.
  • You will be recognised as innovative and enjoy problem solving.
  • You will possess a fundamental undestanding of electranics, machanical engineering and physics.
  • You will excel at and enjoy research.
  • You will have excellent managemanet skills with proven experience working with technical and non-technical people.

Desirable skills and experience:

  • Must have excellent experience in implementing and maintaining distributed networking.
  • Deep commercial experience (7+ years).
  • Must have specific domain expertise of large production Wide Area Networks. This role requires a strong background in OSI Layers 2, 3, 4 and interations with layers up to 7, in particular routing, switching, gateways, proxies and application switching technologies.
  • Should be proficient and confortable working across multiple platforms and OS's, including physical computing and mobile devices (including embedded).
  • Have experience mentoring technical team.
  • Be well versed in assisting with planning, roadmap creation and delivary as well as providing input into the overall strategic direction of the network.
  • Ensure that the network is fully documented and archieving optimum levels of performance.
  • Provide technical expertise to internal application development teams.
  • Posess Excellent persentation skills.
